Our Process

01

Design

We complete your custom FAQ, conversion chart (links to the relevant ecommerce or conversion pages), DMG (Decision Making Guidelines) and account activation.

02

Build

Your lead inbox & outbox are built. Your inbox is a database where leads are categorized according to source channel. Your outbox is an email system for your team to answer issues outside of the FAQ.

03

Communicate

No matter which platform the conversation begins on, we thoughtfully communicate with each user, answering their questions and following best practices while maintaining your unique brand identity and voice.

04

Convert

As we connect with new potential customers we strategically convert them to followers on the next logical channel or to directly purchase on an ecommerce channel.

Full Story of Cards 2.0 Kit

Cards 2.0 are Symbol based Framework/UI Kit for quick design or wireframe of your next landing page project. 100 symbols & elements ready for design or hi-fidelity wireframe for any UI/UX designers or developers out there.

The main goal of this project is to help you - Webflow users - with creating beautiful landing pages or wireframes within minutes directly in Webflow with ready to be published code.
